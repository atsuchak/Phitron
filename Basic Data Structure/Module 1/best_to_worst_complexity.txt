n = 1000

Constant:         O(1)          -> 1
Linear:           O(n)          -> 1000
Logarithmic:      O(logn)       -> 10
Sqrt:             O(sqrt(n))    -> 32
Quadratic:        O(n*n)        -> 1000000
Linearithmic:     O(nlogn)      -> 10000

O(1) > O(logn) > O(sqrt(n)) > O(n) > O(nlogn) > O(n*n)
